LatentChat

Chat privately with your language models.

A personal and offline AI assistant optimized for iPhone. It comes with Llama, but you can bring your own models as well.

LatentChat helps you run language models in GGUF format, with support for some of the most popular models like Llama and Qwen.

It also includes RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), allowing you to easily add custom knowledge in text format.

This feature helps models reduce hallucinations, a common issue with smaller LLMs.

LatentChat is a one-time purchase that provides unlimited access with no subscriptions. Use it privately and offline on your device for as long as you want.

Optimized for iPhone, LatentChat has been tested on iPhone 11 and newer. It also works on iPad, with UI improvements coming soon.
